03 — Outreach & Qualification

How We Sign New Partners

Every new partner goes through our 5-touch qualification process — disciplined enough to protect both sides, fast enough to close within two weeks.

Touch 1
Day 1
Initial Outreach

Personalised email or LinkedIn DM referencing the property's TripAdvisor score, a specific property attribute, and the preferred-rate value proposition. Subject: "A partnership that pays your guests back."

Touch 2
Day 4
Follow-Up + One-Pager

Attach the Butler Button Supplier Overview PDF. Highlight the ROI frame: "Our clients' hotel savings typically cover their Butler subscription cost — making you the reason the trip was affordable."

Touch 3
Day 8
Discovery Call (30 min)

Qualify using BANT-Plus (see below). Confirm: decision-maker, rate authority, minimum discount floor, blackout dates, and appetite for co-marketing. Record call notes in CRM same day.

Touch 4
Day 10
Term Sheet

Send a 1-page term sheet with proposed tier (Preferred/Premier/Signature), net rate commitment, campaign inclusions, and contract term. Aim for a 48-hour turnaround window.

Touch 5
Day 14
Sign & Welcome

DocuSign the Supplier Agreement. Trigger Welcome Launch campaign sequence within 72 hours of signature. Partner goes live in the Butler system within one week.

BANT-Plus Qualification Gates

All five gates must clear before issuing a term sheet.

B
Budget Authority

Can the contact commit to net rates without GM or ownership sign-off? If not, identify and loop in the right person within Touch 2.

A
Authority

Is this a decision-maker or a gatekeeper? Gatekeepers are useful — work with them to book the right meeting, not around them.

N
Network Fit

Does the property or destination align with current demand patterns from our US/UK/Canada client base? Check the demand heat map before the call.

T
Timeline

Can they activate within 30 days? Partners that need 90+ days for internal approvals are logged as "future pipeline" and re-engaged at 60 days.

+
Rate Floor

Will they commit to at least 10% below public rack rate on a net basis? This is the minimum for inclusion in the preferred-rate client pitch. No floor = no listing.

Welcome Launch — Step by Step

The first campaign every partner gets. Runs over 4 phases from contract signing to 30-day review.

Phase 1
Days 1–3
Asset Collection

Request 8–12 high-res property photos, a 150-word property bio, key amenities list, unique selling points, and confirmation of the Butler-exclusive rate. Creative brief sent to partner for approval within 24 hours.

Phase 2
Days 4–6
Asset Creation

Design team produces: 1× announcement email template, 3× social graphics (16:9 + 1:1 + story), 1× blog snippet, and internal Butler team briefing note. All assets sent to partner for sign-off — 48-hour approval window.

Phase 3
Days 7–9
Go Live

Email announcement sent to full subscriber list (typically Thursday 10am local). Social posts scheduled across Instagram, LinkedIn, and Facebook. Butler website listing goes live. Internal Slack announcement to all advisors.

Phase 4
Day 30
30-Day Review

Partner receives a campaign report: email open rate, click rate, social reach, direct enquiries generated, and any bookings attributed to the launch campaign. Sets the baseline for ongoing performance reporting.
