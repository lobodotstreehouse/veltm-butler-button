Common questions

Everything you actually want to know

I'm not Indian. Is this a trip planner for me?

Yes. Our typical India client is a Western traveler — honeymooners, wildlife photographers, heritage-trip families, NRI returnees bringing Western partners. The directory depth means you get rates a walk-in couldn't touch.

Do I pay in rupees?

No. Every quote you see is in USD, and checkout auto-detects your IP for local-currency display. Hotel rates are paid by us on your behalf in INR at the negotiated rate, then reconciled into your USD invoice. You never convert anything.

Do you own any of these hotels?

No. Butler Button is not a hotel operator. Eight Continents, Sterling, Bergamont, TSG, Snow Valley — they're all contracted supplier partners. We negotiate rates for our clients; the properties run themselves.

How is $25/day possible against $5,000 retainer advisors?

AI does the planning shift that used to take a human advisor 8 hours. A human advisor does the reviewing and execution shift. Our preferred-rate buying power means the fee is often paid back by night 2 or 3.

What if I want a Taj or Oberoi?

We book them. Those are on standard published rates — no preferred contract, but no markup either. The fee doesn't change. Luxury-chain properties are typically added to Rajasthan and Kerala trips around our preferred-rate properties.
