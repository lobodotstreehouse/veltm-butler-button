Common questions

What people ask before booking Maldives

Can Butler Button get rates at the top-tier resorts?

Yes for most. The named luxury flagships (Soneva, Cheval Blanc, Four Seasons) are typically published-rate bookings — no discount, but no advisor retainer either. For the tier just below, preferred-rate contracts are the norm.

Is this cheaper than booking direct on the resort site?

Usually yes, and always at least the same. Resort direct rates include a visible markup; our contracted rate typically beats it by 10–25%. Plus pre-arrival room category upgrades when available.

What does the $25/day actually cover in Maldives?

Trip planning, transfer booking, restaurant reservations at in-resort dining, seaplane scheduling, rebooking if weather delays you, in-trip WhatsApp concierge for anything you need while there. If nothing goes wrong, you won't feel it — which is the point.

Do I still tip the resort staff?

Yes. The Butler Button fee is to us, not to resort staff. We'll give you a tipping guide specific to Maldives convention before you arrive.
