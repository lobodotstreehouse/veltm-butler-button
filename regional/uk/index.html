Common questions

Before you book UK & Ireland

Can you beat Booking.com on a London Hilton?

No, and we won't try — the big chains are published-rate for everyone. Where we earn our keep is on the 40% of your trip outside London: the country hotels, the Highlands stay, the Irish manor. The ones that don't show up on Booking.com anyway.

What about driver-guides vs. renting a car?

For Scotland and Ireland, driver-guides are worth it — the roads are thin, the pubs serve real beer, and you'll want to drink the whisky. We'll quote both options in your draft so you can decide.

Does the fee include restaurant bookings?

Yes. Unlimited, while you're on trip. Including Michelin bookings in London and Edinburgh, the gastropub lineup in the Cotswolds, and the Dublin seafood the guidebooks miss.

I'm British. Does this still make sense for me?

For UK domestic trips, probably not — your $25/day fee is best spent on international trips where the logistics are harder. Americans, Canadians, Australians planning UK trips get the most out of this service.
