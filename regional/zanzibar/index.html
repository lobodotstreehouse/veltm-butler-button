Common questions

Before you book East Africa

Can I do Kenya instead of (or with) Tanzania?

Yes. Masai Mara, Laikipia, Amboseli — we plan the Kenyan side too. Zanzibar combos equally well with a Kenya safari, especially via Nairobi connections.

What about peak migration months?

July–October for river crossings in Mara/northern Serengeti; December–March for the calving season in southern Serengeti. We'll match your dates to the best migration experience for when you can travel.

Do I need a visa?

Most Western passports need a Tanzania visa (obtainable on arrival or via e-visa). Your advisor will tell you exactly what to do based on your passport.

What's the worst that can happen on a safari day?

A vehicle breakdown, a bush-flight delay, or a camp miscommunication. Our 8-Hour concierge can reach the ground operator, arrange a replacement, and keep the rest of the itinerary intact — while you're still having tea.
