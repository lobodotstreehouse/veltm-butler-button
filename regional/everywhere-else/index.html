When we'd tell you NOT to book us

The honest don't-bother list

Because a trusted advisor says no when no is the right answer.

You're booking a single chain hotel in a city you know

Book direct. The chain loyalty program will serve you better than a $25/day planning fee. We'd tell you the same thing on a sales call.

You're an experienced DIY traveler who enjoys the planning

Then we're not for you. Butler Button is for people who would rather use that time on something else. If planning is the fun part for you, keep enjoying it.

Your trip is under 3 days or under $800 total

The advisor fee math stops working under a certain trip size. We'll tell you this honestly during the preference survey and suggest you plan it yourself.

You need flight-only or hotel-only booking

We're not a booking engine — we're a planning service. Go to Kayak or Hotels.com for single-component bookings.
