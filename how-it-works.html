Common questions

What counts as a "request"?

Anything reasonable a knowledgeable local friend could help with: reservations, bookings, directions, language help, deliveries, recommendations, re-arrangements. There's no cap on requests during your active Butler hours.

How fast does my Butler respond?

Most requests get a response within minutes. For complex tasks like securing a fully-booked restaurant or arranging a same-day delivery, your Butler will keep you updated while they work on it.

What if my Butler can't help with something?

Your Butler will always tell you clearly and suggest the best alternative path. They won't leave you hanging. See our full services page for the honest list of what's in and out of scope.

Can I use the same Butler on future trips?

Yes — you can request your previous Butler when you book. They'll already know your preferences and travel style, which makes every trip smoother than the last.
