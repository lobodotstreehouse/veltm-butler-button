Concierge Basics

What is a concierge, exactly?

A concierge is someone who handles the stuff you don't have time for. Think hard-to-get dinner reservations, rebooking a flight that just got cancelled, or finding a great guide in a city you've never been to. Butler Button is a travel concierge. We plan your trip, we run it while you're on it, and a real person makes sure nothing falls apart.

Isn't that just a hotel concierge?

Not really. A hotel concierge only helps you while you're at their hotel. Your Butler goes with you the whole trip. Airports, transfers, restaurants, tours, everything. We don't stop helping when you check out.

Who is this for?

Anyone who'd rather enjoy their trip than stress about running it. Busy working folks. Families with kids and grandparents in tow. Honeymooners. First-timers in a country they don't know. And anyone who got burned by a bad Airbnb once and doesn't want to roll the dice again.

What does Butler Button actually do for me?

We plan your trip, book it, and run it. That means building the itinerary, finding flights and hotels, grabbing dinner reservations, setting up guides and drivers, fixing things when they go wrong, and having a real person you can call when your bag doesn't show up at 2 a.m.

What are my options?

Three ways to use us. Trip Planning ($25 per country): a full day-by-day plan, written by AI and checked by a real person, delivered in 24 hours. 8-Hour Butler ($25 a day): a dedicated human on call during the day. 24-Hour Butler ($100 a day): someone on call around the clock for bigger trips, family vacations, or anything you really can't afford to mess up.

So is this an AI thing or a human thing?

Both. The AI is fast. It checks 200,000+ options, figures out the best routes, compares live prices in seconds. The humans are smart. They call hotels, read the room, and make judgment calls a computer can't. AI plans it. A human handles it. You enjoy it.

Is this one of those fancy lifestyle management clubs?

Not really. Those clubs charge tens of thousands a year to book hard-to-get stuff for rich people. We do the same kind of work (private flights, great hotels, tough reservations, VIP stuff) but we start at $25. Same results, without the velvet rope.

What kinds of things will my Butler do?

Whatever the trip needs. Book the table. Chase down your missing bag. Rebook the flight you missed. Translate with your driver. Make sure the boat's fueled and waiting. Find a pediatrician in the middle of the night. Pull off a surprise for your anniversary. If it's travel-related and it could ruin your day, that's Butler stuff.

Can you book a private plane?

Yes. We can set up private aviation through our partners, usually for trips with tricky routing, remote spots, or times when regular flight schedules just don't work. Ask your Butler and we'll get you a quote.
