How Butler Button Works

Where do you operate?

Everywhere. Butler Button covers 195+ countries in 150+ languages, with a directory of 500+ preferred-rate suppliers and local partners on the ground. Our deepest coverage spans India, the UK and Ireland, Tanzania and Zanzibar, and the Maldives — but wherever you're going, there's a Butler who knows the terrain.

What budget do I need?

There is no minimum. Butler Button starts at $25 per country for a full AI-drafted, human-refined trip plan. Most clients spend between $75 and $300 on planning for a full vacation — a fraction of a traditional travel agent's retainer, and often less than what our preferred-supplier pricing saves you on a single hotel booking.

What are your fees?

Flat and transparent. $25 per country for Trip Planning, $25/day for an 8-Hour Butler on the ground, $100/day for a 24-Hour Butler. No percentage of your spend, no commission markups, no surprise line items on the invoice.

Do you only offer full trip planning?

No — you pick the level of support. Some travelers want a full itinerary drafted end-to-end; others just want a Butler on call during a single day in Rome. Start with a $25 plan, add 8-Hour Butler days where you want a human presence, scale up to a 24-Hour Butler for the stretches where you want nothing to go wrong. Mix and match per trip.

Can you help us find hotels, flights, and experiences?

Absolutely — it's the core of what we do. Our AI scans 200,000+ options in real time; our human advisors refine the shortlist and tap our preferred-supplier directory for 10–59% negotiated discounts you won't find on public booking sites. If you've already booked a hotel, just tell us — we'll build the rest of the trip around it.

How involved do we need to be?

Entirely up to you. Answer a 3-minute preference survey and we'll handle the rest, or stay in the loop and approve every detail. Either way, you get a single point of contact, real-time status on your trip, and the ability to ping your Butler 24/7.

Concierge Basics

What is a concierge service?

A concierge handles the things you don't have time to handle yourself — from booking a hard-to-get restaurant to rerouting a missed flight. Butler Button is a travel-focused concierge: we plan your trip, we execute it in real time, and a human on the ground makes sure nothing falls through the cracks.

Is a hotel concierge the same thing?

Not quite. A hotel concierge helps during your stay at one property. Butler Button travels with you — across countries, airports, transfers, restaurants, activities, and anything in between. Your Butler doesn't clock out when you check out.

Who is Butler Button for?

Anyone who'd rather spend their trip on the trip instead of managing it. Busy professionals, multi-generational families, honeymooners, first-time visitors to unfamiliar countries, and travelers who've been burned by a bad booking and don't want to gamble twice.

What does Butler Button actually do?

Plans your trip, books it, and runs it. That means: itinerary design, flight and hotel research, restaurant reservations, guide and driver coordination, last-minute rebookings, translation support, and a human you can call when something goes sideways at 2am local time.

What types of Butler services do you offer?

Three tiers. Trip Planning ($25/country) — AI-drafted, human-refined itinerary delivered in 24 hours. 8-Hour Butler ($25/day) — a dedicated human on call during waking hours. 24-Hour Butler ($100/day) — round-the-clock coverage for complex trips, families, or anything high-stakes.

Is Butler Button an AI concierge?

It's both. Our AI does what AI does best — scan 200,000+ options, optimize routes, compare live prices. Our humans do what humans do best — call a hotel manager, read a situation, make judgment calls. AI plans it. A human handles it. You enjoy it.

What is luxury lifestyle management, and is this that?

Traditional luxury lifestyle management charges five-figure retainers for private-jet access, sold-out tickets, and invitation-only rooms. Butler Button delivers the useful parts of that experience — private aviation, preferred-rate hotels, hard-to-get reservations, VIP access — at prices that start at $25. Same outcomes, none of the gatekeeping.

What does a Butler do?

Whatever the trip needs. Book the table, intercept the delayed bag, rebook the missed connection, translate with the driver, confirm the private boat is fueled, find a pediatrician at midnight, arrange a last-minute anniversary surprise. If it's travel-related and it would otherwise ruin your day, your Butler handles it.

Can you arrange private aviation?

Yes. Butler Button arranges private aviation through our partner network on request — typically for multi-leg itineraries, remote destinations, or when commercial schedules don't fit the trip. Ask your Butler for a quote.
