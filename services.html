What Your Butler Can Do

From securing a fully-booked restaurant to running an errand while you're in a meeting — your Butler is a knowledgeable local who acts. Here's what that looks like in practice.

✈

Travel & Transport

When plans change — and they always do — your Butler re-routes you without the panic.

  • Book last-minute hotels when your original falls through
  • Rearrange missed connections and rebooking flights
  • Arrange airport transfers, taxis, and private cars
  • Find local transport options that aren't on tourist maps
  • Track delayed luggage and liaise with airlines
  • Book trains, ferries, and intercity travel
🍴

Dining & Restaurants

Your Butler knows which restaurants are worth the wait — and how to get you in when they're fully booked.

  • Reserve at fully-booked restaurants via direct contact
  • Order room service in foreign languages on your behalf
  • Get chef recommendations suited to your dietary needs
  • Arrange special occasion setups: flowers, cakes, surprises
  • Find late-night dining when hotel kitchens have closed
  • Secure tables at pop-ups, chef's counters, and hard-to-find spots
🚚

Errands & Deliveries

The service card concierges explicitly exclude — your Butler can physically run errands and arrange deliveries.

  • Forgotten items retrieved from previous hotels
  • Phone chargers, adapters, or essentials delivered to your room
  • Gifts and flowers sent to your hotel room or a contact
  • Prescription collection from local pharmacies
  • Send packages or souvenirs back home
  • Dry cleaning collection and return coordination
📍

Local Knowledge

Not what TripAdvisor says. What a knowledgeable local actually recommends.

  • Neighbourhood-specific recommendations off the tourist trail
  • Translation help for menus, signs, and conversations
  • Local emergency contacts: hospitals, police, consulates
  • Cultural guidance: etiquette, tipping, what to avoid
  • Pharmacy and medical service locations
  • Real-time advice on local conditions, strikes, or closures
📋

Planning & Itineraries

Before you arrive or on the fly — your Butler helps you make the most of every day.

  • Day-by-day itinerary tailored to your travel style
  • Activity and experience bookings
  • Permit and timed-entry queue management
  • Private guide sourcing in your language
  • Multi-city trip sequencing and logistics
  • Real-time itinerary adjustments when plans shift
🛠

Emergency Support

When something goes wrong abroad, having someone in your corner makes the difference between panic and resolution.

  • Lost passport: guidance, contacts, emergency travel docs
  • Medical referrals to English-speaking clinics and doctors
  • Urgent flight and hotel rebookings after disruptions
  • Lost wallet or card: local resources and next steps
  • Safety advice in unfamiliar situations
  • Contacting family or colleagues on your behalf

Unlike card concierge services, your Butler can run in-person errands and handle deliveries.
American Express, Chase, and most card concierges explicitly exclude physical errands from their scope.
Your Butler does not.

Your Butler cannot

Honesty matters. Here's what falls outside scope — not to disappoint, but because we'd rather be clear than overpromise.

  • Provide legal advice or act as a legal representative
  • Administer medical treatment or prescribe medication
  • Assist with anything illegal in the destination country
  • Act as a licensed financial adviser
  • Make formal insurance claims on your behalf
  • Provide childcare or supervise minors unattended
  • Drive you personally (though they can arrange drivers)
  • Guarantee entry to venues beyond their control

If you're unsure whether something is in scope, just ask — your Butler will always give you an honest answer and suggest the best available path forward.
