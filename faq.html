Trip Planning

How does trip planning work?
You complete a detailed preference survey. Our AI scans 200,000+ options. A certified human advisor reviews and refines the results into a complete itinerary, delivered within 24 hours. Average: 3 minutes to request, 24 hours to receive.
How much does trip planning cost?
$25 per country in your itinerary. A 3-country trip costs $75. No hidden fees, no membership.
Who reviews my itinerary?
Every itinerary is reviewed by a certified travel advisor who has personally traveled to your destination. Average: 9 years of destination-specific expertise.
Can I make changes after receiving my itinerary?
Yes. Your itinerary is a starting point built around your preferences. Revisions are included.
Is this just an AI chatbot generating itineraries?
No. AI handles the search and synthesis. A human advisor with destination expertise makes the final recommendations and reviews every detail. The difference: AI scans 200,000 options. A human who's been there picks the right ones.

Concierge Service

How quickly does my concierge respond?
Average response time is under 4 minutes, 24/7 for the 24-Hour tier. The 8-Hour tier covers morning to night in your travel timezone.
Is this a chatbot or a real person?
100% human, first contact. Every time. No automated chatbot on any channel. Your advisor studies your full itinerary before you depart.
Do I get the same advisor for my whole trip?
Yes. Advisor continuity: the same person is assigned and available for the full duration of your trip.
What can my concierge actually do?
Restaurant reservations, transport booking, local intelligence, in-person errands, emergency rebooking, medical referrals, gift delivery, translation assistance, and permit management. 97% of disruptions are resolved within 60 minutes.
What can my concierge NOT do?
Legal advice, medical treatment, anything illegal, and financial transactions. We are transparent about scope.
Can I cancel mid-trip?
Yes. Pay only for days used. No cancellation fees.
Are recommendations independent?
100%. Butler Button has no affiliate agreements and no commercial deals with hotels or operators. Every recommendation is independent.
