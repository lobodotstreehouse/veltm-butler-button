During active service hours, your Butler responds within 15 minutes — usually much faster. For the 24-Hour Butler tier, your Butler is available around the clock, including overnight and across time zones. For the 8-Hour Butler tier, response windows are set at the start of your service day so you always know when your Butler is on.

If you send a message outside your active window on the 8-Hour plan, your Butler will pick it up at the start of the next service period and respond promptly.

Your Butler is a concierge, not a lawyer, doctor, or financial adviser. There are a small number of things outside the scope of service:

  • Legal advice — your Butler can help you find a local lawyer, but cannot give legal counsel
  • Medical treatment — your Butler can locate doctors, clinics, and pharmacies, but cannot provide medical care
  • Anything illegal — full stop
  • Financial transactions — your Butler cannot make payments, transfer funds, or access financial accounts on your behalf

If you're ever unsure whether something falls within scope, just ask — your Butler will tell you honestly what they can and can't do.

No app required. Butler Button works entirely through text messaging — SMS, WhatsApp, or whatever messaging platform you already use. Nothing to download, nothing to set up. It works on any phone, in any country, on any network.

This is intentional. When you're in an unfamiliar country at 2am with a flat battery, the last thing you need is an app that requires a login. Your Butler is one message away.

Absolutely. Butler Button supports 150+ languages. Your Butler will communicate with you in your language — not just translate, but respond naturally and fluently. When dealing with local services in-country, your Butler also handles translation on your behalf, so language is never a barrier.

This is one of the areas where Butler Button significantly outperforms card concierge services and most luxury concierge providers, who typically operate in 13–35 languages.

If you're on the 24-Hour Butler tier ($100/day), your Butler is available around the clock — including 3am, across any time zone. This tier is designed precisely for situations like overnight emergencies, red-eye disruptions, and multi-timezone trips.

If you're on the 8-Hour Butler tier ($25/day), your service operates within a defined daytime window. For trips where overnight coverage matters — solo travel, international itineraries, or any trip where things could go sideways — we recommend upgrading to the 24-Hour tier for peace of mind.

Both tiers give you a real human Butler — the difference is coverage window and complexity of support:

  • 8-Hour Butler ($25/day) — Daytime coverage, ideal for planned itineraries, day trips, and travellers who don't anticipate needing overnight support. Best for single-destination trips with a clear agenda.
  • 24-Hour Butler ($100/day) — Round-the-clock availability. Ideal for complex multi-destination trips, business travel, solo travel, family travel, and any scenario where unexpected situations could arise at any hour.

You can also mix tiers across different days of a trip — paying $25 on low-key days and $100 on travel days with early flights or late arrivals.

Travel agents plan trips. Butler Button plans and executes — in real time, during your actual trip.

A travel agent books your flights and hotel, hands you a PDF, and wishes you well. If something goes wrong once you land, you're on your own. Your Butler, by contrast, is with you throughout the journey. They rebook your disrupted flight while you're still at the gate. They call the restaurant when the reservation falls through. They physically collect what you need from a local shop.

Think of a travel agent as a one-time planner. Think of your Butler as a personal assistant who travels with you — without taking up a seat.
