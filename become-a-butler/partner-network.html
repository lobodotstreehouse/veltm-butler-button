Butler Partner Network

When you join VELTM, you don't just gain a platform — you gain access to a global network of supplier partnerships built over years of consistent, high-volume bookings. Here's what that means for you and your travelers.

The VELTM Partner Network is the infrastructure that makes great Butler service possible at scale. Travelers don't just want a human on the other end of their texts — they want a human who can actually deliver things that the average person can't arrange on their own. The partner network is how Butlers deliver that.

Who Is in the Network?

VELTM has established formal partner relationships across every major travel category:

  • Hotels and resorts: Independent luxury properties, boutique collections, and select chain brands across 195+ countries. Partner hotels are vetted for quality, responsiveness, and their track record with VELTM travelers.
  • Restaurants and dining experiences: From Michelin-starred establishments to beloved neighborhood institutions — relationships built with reservation managers and owners who hold tables for VELTM requests.
  • Tour operators and DMCs: Destination Management Companies (DMCs) and specialist tour operators in every major region, curated for quality, safety standards, and local expertise.
  • Transportation providers: Ground transportation networks, private aviation contacts, yacht charter brokers, and rail booking specialists across key markets.
  • Experience and activity operators: Vetted operators for everything from Antarctic expeditions to private cooking classes — with operator certifications and insurance standards verified by VELTM.

How Butlers Benefit from Partner Rates

VELTM's consolidated booking volume gives the network negotiating power that individual Butlers — or even large traditional travel agencies — can't replicate alone. That power translates directly into your ability to deliver value.

  • Preferential rates: Partner hotels offer VELTM Butlers rates below publicly available prices. This means you can often find your traveler a better price than they'd get booking directly — which is a powerful thing to be able to offer.
  • Value-adds and amenities: Many partner properties offer automatic amenity packages for VELTM bookings — room upgrades when available, daily breakfast, early check-in, late checkout, or a property credit. These extras can significantly enhance the traveler's experience at no additional cost.
  • Commission on bookings: When you book through a partner, VELTM earns a commission that contributes to Butler earnings. The partner rate model means travelers pay less and you earn more — a genuine win on both sides.
  • Priority access: Partner operators and restaurants extend priority status to VELTM requests. When a hotel has two rooms left and two booking requests arrive simultaneously, VELTM partners give our Butlers first option.

Direct Supplier Relationships

Beyond rates and commissions, the partner network gives you something harder to quantify but even more valuable: direct relationships with decision-makers at key suppliers.

  • Named contacts: The VELTM partner directory lists specific contacts at each supplier — the duty manager at a hotel, the reservations director at a restaurant group, the operations lead at a tour operator. These are real people who know the VELTM name and will pick up when you call.
  • Escalation paths: When a standard request needs to go above the front-line team, the partner directory gives you the direct line. No hold music, no explaining who VELTM is — just a productive conversation with someone who can say yes.
  • Partner pages in the platform: Each partner has a dedicated page in the Butler platform with property descriptions, booking instructions, contact hierarchy, special notes (e.g., "property is adult-only," "currently renovating east wing"), and past traveler feedback from other Butlers.

Growing the Network

The partner network is not static. Butlers play an active role in expanding it:

  • Recommend new partners: If you've worked with a supplier who consistently delivers exceptional service, nominate them for the VELTM partner program through the platform. Approved partners strengthen your toolkit and the entire Butler community's.
  • Flag underperforming partners: If a listed partner repeatedly fails to deliver, your feedback matters. The partnership team reviews performance data regularly and removes partners who don't maintain VELTM's standards.
  • Build your own supplier relationships: The network is the foundation, but your personal relationships with specific suppliers in your areas of specialty are your professional edge. Nurture them. They're part of what makes you irreplaceable.

The partner network is ultimately about one thing: giving you the ability to say yes to your travelers more often, more confidently, and at a better value than they could find on their own. That's the Butler difference.
