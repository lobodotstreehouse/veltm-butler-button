Butler Platform: Latest Features

The VELTM Butler platform is a living product, continuously improved based on feedback from Butlers and travelers. Here's a look at the five most impactful recent additions to your toolkit.

VELTM ships platform updates on a rolling basis, with major feature releases announced through the Butler community digest and detailed in the platform's changelog. Below are the five features that have had the most significant impact on how Butlers work — each one designed to reduce friction, improve traveler experience, and give you more time to focus on what only a human can do.

Feature 1: Maps Integration

The new Maps Integration brings spatial context directly into the request workflow. When you're working on an itinerary or researching options for a traveler, geography matters enormously — and having to toggle between the platform and an external mapping tool was a persistent friction point.

  • Itinerary mapping: Any itinerary built in the platform now generates an automatic visual map showing all bookings, venues, and waypoints plotted geographically. Share the map view with travelers so they can immediately visualize their trip.
  • Proximity search: When researching dining or activity options for a traveler, filter by proximity to their hotel or a specific address. "Three great restaurants within a 10-minute walk of the Uffizi" is now a five-second search, not a five-minute one.
  • Transfer time estimates: Maps integration automatically calculates realistic travel times between itinerary points, flagging any segments where the scheduled time is tighter than traffic conditions typically allow. No more itineraries that look fine on paper but fall apart on the ground.
  • Offline access: Travelers can download their itinerary map for offline access — critical in destinations with limited mobile data coverage.

Feature 2: Enhanced Client Lead System

The client lead system received a significant update, with improvements to both matching accuracy and Butler control over lead preferences.

  • Lead preference filters: Butlers can now set granular preferences for the types of leads they want to receive — by destination region, request category, trip duration, and traveler tier. The algorithm respects these preferences while still offering leads outside your filters when you're the best available match.
  • Lead preview: Before accepting a competitive pool lead, you now see a brief summary of the request — destination, dates, approximate complexity, and traveler tier — allowing you to make an informed accept/decline decision without opening the full request.
  • Match score transparency: Each lead now shows your personal match score for that request, so you understand why you were selected and what strengths the algorithm is recognizing in you for this particular traveler.

See the full Client Lead System guide for a comprehensive overview.

Feature 3: Smart Pricing

One of the most common questions new Butlers ask is: "How do I price my services?" Smart Pricing takes the guesswork out of it.

  • Market rate benchmarking: For any service category and destination combination, Smart Pricing shows the current range of rates being charged by VELTM Butlers globally — helping you position your rates competitively without undervaluing your expertise.
  • Dynamic recommendations: Based on your experience level, specialty flags, satisfaction score, and the complexity of a specific request, Smart Pricing suggests an appropriate rate for the engagement. You're always free to override it — it's a recommendation, not a constraint.
  • Traveler price sensitivity signals: For travelers whose booking history indicates strong price sensitivity, Smart Pricing will flag this context — useful when deciding whether to present a premium option or lead with a value-oriented approach.
  • Transparent fee breakdown: When generating a quote for a traveler, the platform now produces a clear itemized breakdown that travelers find reassuring — no mystery line items, no surprises at checkout.

Feature 4: Favorites and Lists

Every experienced Butler develops a mental list of trusted suppliers, favorite properties, and go-to restaurants across key destinations. Favorites and Lists brings that knowledge into the platform where it can be organized, shared, and acted on.

  • Personal favorites: Save any supplier, property, restaurant, or experience operator to a personal favorites list with private notes. "Best for families," "always ask for Marco," "avoid in August — renovation" — your institutional knowledge, captured.
  • Curated lists for travelers: Build and share curated lists directly with travelers. "My top 5 restaurants in Lisbon" or "Best boutique hotels in the Cotswolds under £400/night" can be sent as a formatted, shareable link — a polished, professional deliverable that takes minutes to create.
  • Community lists: Browse lists created and shared by other Butlers in the community. A specialist Butler's list of expedition operators in Patagonia is a shortcut worth hours of research.
  • Live availability links: Lists can include live availability links for partner properties and operators, so travelers can see real-time availability directly from the list you share.

Feature 5: Traveler Profiles

Great service is built on knowing your traveler. The enhanced Traveler Profiles feature gives you a comprehensive, structured view of each client — consolidating everything you know and everything VELTM knows into a single reference.

  • Preference library: Dietary requirements, hotel preferences, seat preferences, loyalty program numbers, travel document details, emergency contacts — all stored and accessible at a glance when a new request arrives.
  • Trip history: A full record of every engagement the traveler has had through VELTM, including which Butlers served them, what was requested, and any notes from prior engagements.
  • Relationship timeline: A chronological view of your interactions with a specific traveler — useful for context when a traveler returns after a gap and you want to reconnect genuinely rather than starting from scratch.
  • Occasion tracker: Log birthdays, anniversaries, and special occasions. The platform sends you a reminder 30 days out — giving you time to suggest something meaningful before the traveler even thinks to ask.

Platform updates are announced in the Butler community digest every month. Ambassador Butlers receive early access to beta features — another reason to build toward Ambassador status.
