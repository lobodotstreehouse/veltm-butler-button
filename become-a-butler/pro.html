Butler Pro Program

Already a travel professional? The Butler Pro Program is designed for experienced travel agents, hotel concierges, destination specialists, and corporate travel managers who want to bring their expertise to the VELTM platform — on better terms.

The travel industry is full of exceptional professionals who know destinations, suppliers, and travelers better than anyone — but who are working within structures that limit their earning potential, their flexibility, or both. Butler Pro was built for them. If you have genuine travel expertise and an established track record, you shouldn't have to start from the bottom of any platform. Butler Pro gets you started where your experience actually places you.

Who Butler Pro Is For

The Pro program is specifically designed for travel professionals transitioning to or adding VELTM to their practice:

  • Independent travel agents and advisors with an established client base and documented booking history looking for a platform that provides leads, tooling, and supplier relationships without requiring agency affiliation.
  • Hotel and resort concierges with deep destination knowledge and supplier relationships developed through years of on-the-ground service — who want to take that expertise independent.
  • Corporate travel managers with expertise in complex itineraries, duty of care, and business travel logistics — skills that translate powerfully into the premium segment of VELTM's traveler base.
  • Destination specialists and DMC alumni with deep regional expertise in specific countries or regions and existing relationships with local operators and suppliers.
  • Tour directors and expedition leaders who have led groups through complex logistics in challenging destinations and want to build an independent practice around that experience.

How to Qualify for Butler Pro

Butler Pro entry requires verification of professional experience. The qualification process is streamlined and typically completed within five business days:

  • Professional history documentation: A CV or LinkedIn profile showing a minimum of two years of full-time professional travel industry experience, or five years part-time.
  • References: Two professional references — from former employers, suppliers, or clients — who can speak to your travel service capabilities.
  • Booking history (for agents): Evidence of prior booking volume — GDS records, agency commission statements, or equivalent documentation. VELTM's Pro team will advise on acceptable formats and treats all documentation confidentially.
  • Skills assessment: A brief practical assessment covering core Butler competencies — not as a barrier, but to ensure you're placed correctly in the platform from day one and to identify any VELTM-specific tools or processes that may be new to you.

Professionals who don't yet meet the Pro threshold are warmly encouraged to apply through the standard Butler onboarding path. Many Pro-eligible Butlers started standard and applied for Pro status after their first six months on the platform.

Butler Pro Benefits

Pro status unlocks a meaningfully different set of terms and advantages than the standard Butler tier:

  • Higher commission rates: Pro Butlers earn a higher base commission on all bookings made through the VELTM partner network — reflecting the experience and conversion quality they bring. Exact rates are discussed during the Pro onboarding conversation.
  • Priority assignment for complex requests: Multi-destination itineraries, long-duration engagements, VIP and celebrity traveler accounts, and high-spend group requests are routed to Pro Butlers first. These are the requests that make the most of deep professional expertise.
  • Dedicated support: Pro Butlers have access to a dedicated VELTM support contact — a real person who knows their profile and history — rather than the general support queue. For time-sensitive situations, this is a significant advantage.
  • Accelerated Ambassador eligibility: The satisfaction score threshold and minimum engagement count required for Ambassador status are reduced for Pro Butlers, reflecting their prior demonstrated expertise.
  • Client migration support: If you're bringing an existing client base to VELTM, the Pro team will help you migrate client information, set up your profile to match your existing brand positioning, and create a communication template for introducing your existing clients to the VELTM platform.
  • Supplier relationship recognition: Existing supplier relationships you bring to VELTM can be formally recognized in the platform — giving your established contacts preferred partner status and you the ability to book through them at VELTM rates.

Transitioning from an Existing Travel Practice

The most common question from prospective Pro Butlers is about transition: how do you move your existing clients and business to a new platform without disruption?

  • Parallel operation: There is no requirement to close your existing practice before joining VELTM. Many Pro Butlers operate on the platform alongside their existing arrangements initially, migrating clients gradually as they become comfortable with the VELTM workflow.
  • Client introduction process: VELTM provides a white-label introduction package that frames VELTM as a platform enhancement to your existing service — not a replacement. Your clients experience better tooling and infrastructure; your expertise and relationship remain the constant.
  • No non-compete constraints: VELTM does not impose non-compete clauses on Butler Pro members. You retain full freedom to manage your professional relationships as you see fit.
  • Agency affiliation flexibility: If you currently hold agency accreditation (IATA, CLIA, ASTA, etc.), VELTM can accommodate bookings that leverage those credentials alongside the VELTM partner network. The Pro team will work through the specifics with you during onboarding.

Ready to Apply?

If you're an experienced travel professional who's been looking for a platform that matches your level, the Butler Pro Program was built for you. The application process begins with a conversation — no forms to fill out, no fees to pay.

Contact the Butler Pro team directly at pro@veltmtours.com or begin your application through the standard Become a Butler form and note your professional background in the experience section. The Pro team reviews all applications and will reach out within two business days.
