VELTM's Three Service Tiers

VELTM offers travelers three service tiers, each with a different price point, scope of service, and corresponding Butler earnings structure. Understanding the economics of each tier helps you make strategic decisions about which engagements to prioritize and how to build a high-earning practice.

Tier 1: Trip Planning — $25 Per Country

Trip Planning is a pre-trip research and itinerary service. A traveler pays $25 per destination country and receives a detailed, personalized itinerary — curated day-by-day plans, restaurant recommendations, logistics notes, local tips, and practical information tailored to their specific travel style and dates.

What the Butler Does

  • Review the traveler's brief and preferences
  • Use Butler AI to generate a research-backed itinerary draft
  • Refine and personalize the draft with local knowledge and judgment
  • Deliver the itinerary via the VELTM platform
  • Handle one round of revision requests if needed

Time Involved

An experienced Butler using AI tools can complete a Trip Planning engagement in 45–90 minutes for a straightforward destination. Complex multi-city itineraries with specific requirements may take 2–3 hours.

Butler Earnings

Butler commission on Trip Planning engagements is structured as a flat fee per completed itinerary. At standard certification, this works out to approximately $8–$12 per itinerary, depending on destination complexity and your rating tier. Advanced-certified Butlers with destination expertise earn at the higher end of this range for their specialty regions.

Best For

Trip Planning is a good starting point for new Butlers building their rating record. The engagements are lower-pressure, entirely asynchronous, and let you develop confidence with the AI tools before moving to live service windows. As a standalone income source, Trip Planning is most attractive at high volume — Butlers doing 20+ itineraries per month treat it as reliable recurring income.

Tier 2: 8-Hour Butler — $25 Per Day

The 8-Hour Butler is a live, real-time concierge service covering an eight-hour window during a traveler's trip. The traveler can text requests throughout the day and the Butler handles them as they come in — reservations, recommendations, logistics coordination, problem-solving, and anything else that arises.

What the Butler Does

  • Open an active service window at the agreed start time
  • Monitor the engagement channel throughout the 8-hour window
  • Handle all incoming traveler requests in real time
  • Proactively flag relevant information (weather, transport changes, reservation reminders)
  • Close the engagement at the end of the window with a summary note

Time Involved

The 8-hour window is the commitment. In practice, active request volume varies — some days are high-intensity with frequent requests; others are quieter. Most Butlers find they can manage light background tasks during lower-intensity periods of an 8-hour engagement.

Butler Earnings

8-Hour engagements combine an engagement commission with an hourly active service rate. Total Butler earnings for a standard 8-Hour engagement typically run $18–$28, depending on certification level, rating tier, and request volume. Advanced-certified Butlers handling premium 8-Hour engagements can earn $30–$40 per engagement.

Best For

The 8-Hour tier is the workhorse of most Butler practices. The price point is accessible to a wide range of travelers, which means strong demand volume. Butlers who structure their availability around consistent 8-Hour windows build steady, predictable income.

Tier 3: 24-Hour Butler — $100 Per Day

The 24-Hour Butler is VELTM's premium service tier. The traveler has a Butler on-call for the full 24-hour period — available from early morning departures to late-night arrivals, from breakfast reservations to after-hours emergencies. This is the service that travelers use for high-stakes trips, complex itineraries, and moments when they want to know that help is always one text away.

What the Butler Does

  • Remain available and responsive for the full 24-hour window
  • Handle all incoming requests across morning, afternoon, and evening periods
  • Proactively monitor the traveler's itinerary for disruptions
  • Manage any emergency or crisis situations that arise
  • Coordinate across multiple vendors and time zones as needed

Time Involved

24-Hour engagements are the most demanding in terms of availability commitment. Most Butlers who accept them build a coverage structure — being highly available during peak travel hours (6am–10pm) and maintaining lighter monitoring during overnight hours. You must be genuinely reachable throughout the window.

Butler Earnings

The 24-Hour tier delivers the highest per-engagement earnings on the platform. Standard-certified Butler earnings for a 24-Hour engagement typically run $55–$80. Advanced-certified Butlers — particularly those with Luxury Specialist or Destination Expert designations — earn $80–$120+ per 24-Hour engagement. Multi-day 24-Hour packages (3 days, 7 days) carry volume bonuses on top of the per-day rate.

Best For

The 24-Hour tier is the fastest path to high per-hour earnings for Butlers who are ready for it. A single 24-Hour engagement can pay more than two full days of Trip Planning work. Butlers who hold Advanced Certification and focus on the premium tier can build a six-figure annual income from a manageable number of high-value engagements.

Tips to Increase Your Per-Engagement Earnings

1. Earn Advanced Certification

The rate premium is real and ongoing. Every premium engagement you handle as an Advanced-certified Butler earns more than the same engagement would at standard certification. The ROI on the certification investment is typically recovered within the first 10–15 premium engagements.

2. Build Toward Direct Requests

When a traveler requests you by name — bypassing the general matching queue — your commission rate is higher than for algorithmically matched engagements. Building a base of direct-request clients is the highest-leverage activity you can invest in as a Butler.

3. Shift Your Tier Mix Over Time

New Butlers naturally start heavy on Trip Planning engagements. As your confidence and ratings grow, deliberately shift toward more 8-Hour and 24-Hour engagements. The same hours of availability generate significantly higher income at the higher tiers.

4. Pursue Multi-Day Packages

Travelers who book 3-day or 7-day 24-Hour packages are the highest-value clients on the platform. The volume bonus on multi-day packages means your per-day earnings are higher than for single-day engagements. When a traveler on a 1-day engagement has a great experience, actively let them know multi-day packages are available for their next trip.

5. Maintain Your Rating Obsessively

Every 0.1 point on your rating affects your matching priority and your rate tier. The difference between a 4.6 and a 4.9 is not marginal — it changes which engagements you are matched with, how prominently your profile appears, and whether you qualify for rating bonuses.
