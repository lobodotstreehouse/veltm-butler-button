What Does It Mean to Be a VELTM Butler?

VELTM Butlers are the human heart of the Butler Button experience. While our AI platform handles trip research, itinerary drafting, and destination intelligence, you are the one who makes it real — handling live requests from travelers in 195+ countries, solving problems on the ground, and delivering the kind of attentive, personal service that no algorithm can replicate.

When a traveler texts "I need a last-minute reservation at the best restaurant in Marrakech tonight," you are the one who makes that happen. When a family's connecting flight gets cancelled in Tokyo, you are the one who stays calm, pivots, and finds a solution. That is the Butler difference — and it is what travelers pay for.

What Butlers Earn

Butler earnings are based on a commission-per-engagement model supplemented by an hourly rate during active service windows. Here is what the numbers look like at different activity levels:

Activity Level Engagements/Month Estimated Monthly Earnings
Light (part-time) 8–12 engagements $400–$800
Moderate 20–30 engagements $1,200–$2,200
Active 40–60 engagements $2,800–$4,500
Full-Time 70+ engagements $5,000–$8,000+

Earnings estimates are based on a mix of service tiers and are not guaranteed. Actual earnings depend on engagement volume, service quality, client ratings, and market demand in your region.

Who Makes a Great Butler?

There is no single background that defines a VELTM Butler. Our network includes former hotel concierges, multilingual freelancers, travel enthusiasts, expats with deep local knowledge, and career changers from hospitality, customer service, and logistics. What they all share is a set of core qualities:

  • Hospitality instinct — You genuinely enjoy helping people and go out of your way to make their experience exceptional.
  • Calm under pressure — Travel disruptions happen. You stay composed, think on your feet, and find solutions.
  • Communication clarity — You write clearly and respond promptly. Travelers depend on you for real-time guidance.
  • Local or destination knowledge — You know a place deeply, or you know how to find the right information fast.
  • Language skills — English is required. Additional languages make you significantly more valuable to our global network.
  • Reliability — When you accept an engagement, you follow through. Travelers are counting on you.

The Application Process

  1. Submit your application

    Tell us about your background, language skills, and the destinations where you have the strongest knowledge. See our Application Tips to put your best foot forward.

  2. Complete the onboarding assessment

    A short skills and situational assessment to understand your service style and problem-solving approach. There are no wrong answers — we want to understand how you think.

  3. Complete Butler Certification

    Our online training program covers platform tools, service standards, AI integration, and cultural competency. Most applicants complete it in 2–4 weeks. See the Certification Program for full details.

  4. Activate your Butler profile

    Once certified, your profile goes live on the VELTM platform. Travelers can request you directly, or our matching system will connect you with the right engagements. See Profile Tips to make yours shine.

  5. Start taking engagements

    Accept your first engagement, deliver exceptional service, collect your rating, and build your reputation. Your income grows as your rating and volume increase.
