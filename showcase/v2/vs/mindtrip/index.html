Frequently Asked

Butler Button vs. Mindtrip.

What's the difference between Butler Button and Mindtrip? +

Mindtrip is an AI trip-planning assistant. It generates itinerary suggestions conversationally and surfaces booking options through partner integrations. It's a planning tool.

Butler Button pairs AI planning with a dedicated human advisor. The AI scans 200,000+ options filtered to your preferences; a certified advisor then reviews the plan, books everything on your behalf, and stays reachable throughout the trip to handle whatever comes up. Mindtrip plans. Butler Button plans and handles.

Is Mindtrip free? +

Mindtrip offers a free tier for AI-generated trip planning, with paid upgrades for premium features as of 2026. Verify current pricing on their site.

Butler Button is $25 per country for an AI-planned, advisor-reviewed itinerary, or $25 per day for the 8-Hour Concierge service. The two are not directly price-comparable because they do different things — Mindtrip plans, Butler Button plans and executes.

Does Mindtrip have human travel advisors? +

No. Mindtrip is an AI-only product. It does not employ human travel advisors, does not provide live concierge service, and does not offer in-trip support.

Butler Button provides both AI-assisted planning and a dedicated human advisor for every trip — with an average of 9 years of on-ground destination expertise. 100% human first contact, every time. No chatbot on any channel.

Can Mindtrip book my flights and hotels? +

Mindtrip surfaces flight and hotel options and integrates with booking partners, but you complete the transaction yourself through the partner site.

Butler Button advisors book flights, hotels, private aviation, restaurants, and ground transport directly on your behalf. You don't click through a partner site — the advisor handles it and sends you confirmations.

Can Mindtrip handle a flight cancellation mid-trip? +

No. Mindtrip is a planning product, not an in-trip service. It can help you research alternatives, but it doesn't call the airline, rebook the hotel, or contact your tour guide.

Butler Button's advisor is reachable during the trip and handles disruption directly. 97% of in-trip disruptions are resolved in under 60 minutes — usually much faster. Your advisor already has your itinerary loaded, so there's no "let me catch you up" step.

Which should I use if I just want trip inspiration? +

Mindtrip works well for early-stage brainstorming at no cost — surfacing destinations, rough itineraries, and ideas through a conversational interface.

Choose Butler Button once you want a vetted plan, bookings executed, and a human reachable during the trip. Many travelers explore with AI tools first, then hand the direction to a Butler Button advisor for the build and execution.

Does Butler Button use AI? +

Yes. Butler Button's AI engine scans 200,000+ flight, stay, experience, and transfer options filtered against your exact preferences — well beyond what any human could review manually.

The difference is what happens next: a certified human advisor with an average of 9 years of destination expertise reviews every recommendation before it reaches you, and executes the booking. AI scales the search. A human makes the call.

Can I use my existing credit card with Butler Button and still earn points? +

Yes. Butler Button is card-agnostic. You tell us which card to charge and we use it for all bookings — the $25/day service fee plus every hotel, excursion, and transport booking. You earn whatever points, miles, or cash back your card offers.

You can also share your rewards goals at checkout — earn points, redeem points, or a hybrid strategy — and your advisor will design your itinerary and booking sequence around your preferred program.

How does Butler Button get prices 30–40% below OTA rates? +

Butler Button's operations center is based in India. When advisors purchase hotels, excursions, and ground transport on your behalf, they do so from Indian IP addresses. Suppliers offer local market pricing at those addresses — rather than the inflated rates that western OTAs push on first-world consumers.

Mindtrip has no purchasing power — it links out to standard consumer OTA pages. The average Butler Button saving is 30–40% on hotels, excursions, and ground transport.

What is Butler Button's commission structure? +

Butler Button operates a no-commission travel agency for clients who pay directly: 0% commission if you pay for travel upfront or on arrival.

If you prefer to be invoiced after your trip, the commission is 10% — below the industry standard of 12–15%. Most clients choose upfront or on-arrival payment to avoid commission entirely.

Does Butler Button donate to charity? +

Yes. Butler Button automatically donates 1% of every purchase to a nonprofit of your choice, powered by our Pledge integration. At checkout you simply pick the beneficiary from over a million registered nonprofits.

No extra steps. No extra cost to you. Butler Button absorbs the donation.
