Trip Planning

Five positioning angles for the trip-planning product experience

V1 · THE FREEDOM ANGLE

Your Trip. Your Way. Finally.

Trip planning that starts with your preferences — and never stops adapting.

You tell us the trip you want. We build every detail around it — flights, stays, experiences, transfers, and everything in between. No cookie-cutter packages. No hours of research. No compromises on the itinerary you actually imagined. Butler Button is the only trip-planning service built entirely around the traveler — not the inventory.

Persona

Alex — Senior Marketing Director, 38

Total control, zero wasted research, reflects his taste, feels seasoned

Proof Points

  • Preference survey, not template
  • 12 hours saved on average
  • 95%+ rate 4–5 stars

Copy in Action

Landing Page
"Tell us what you want. We build it around you — every flight, stay, and moment in between."
Email Subject
"Your perfect trip doesn't have to start with 47 browser tabs"
Social
"No cookie-cutter packages. Just your trip, exactly how you imagined it. #OwnYourJourney"
Ad Headline
"Trip planning that bends to you — not the other way around."

Explore

Traveler's vision drives everything; time savings; sophistication language.

Use: "built around your preferences" · "exactly as you imagined it"

Avoid

Don't say "we'll take care of everything" without anchoring to preferences; no handing-over-control language; don't over-promise.

Avoid: "we know what's best for you" · "leave it all to us"
