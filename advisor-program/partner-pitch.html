How the partnership works

Simple structure.
No hidden mechanics.

Client referrals

You refer your client to Butler Button. We handle the planning, execution, and in-trip concierge. You maintain the relationship and collect the referral commission on each booking.

White-label option

Your clients never see Butler Button. All communications go under your brand. You present the itinerary. We operate invisibly in the background.

Rate access

Partner advisors can quote clients on preferred rates directly during the proposal stage. Pricing is disclosed under advisor NDA — not available on public rate sheets.

Client fee structure

Clients pay $25/day advisory fee (min. $125) plus the net hotel rate. No markup on hotels — the preferred rate is the rate. Your commission is paid by Butler Button, not the client.

VIP concierge tier

For UHNW clients — private aviation access, heads-of-state itineraries, charter packages. Commission rates and supplier access elevated at VIP tier. NDA required.

No minimum volume

No monthly booking quotas. No retainer fee to join. You activate the partnership when you have a client trip that fits. Commission paid on completion of trip.
